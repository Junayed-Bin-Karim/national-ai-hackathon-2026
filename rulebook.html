Eligibility & Team Structure

The competition is designed to foster innovation among students in Bangladesh.

  • Who can apply: Undergraduate students currently enrolled in any university across Bangladesh.
  • Team Size: Minimum 1 member, Maximum 2 members.
  • Location: The event is 100% Online. You can participate from anywhere.
  • Skill Level: Prior AI expertise is NOT required. We look for innovative ideas.
Limit: Only the first 100 teams to register will be accepted.

Round 1: Idea Submission

This is a screening round. No coding is required yet.

Requirements:
  • Create a presentation (PDF or PPTX).
  • Slide 1: Problem Statement (What are you solving?)
  • Slide 2: Proposed AI Solution.
  • Slide 3: Expected Real-world Impact in Bangladesh.
  • Optional: Tech stack or implementation plan.

Note: You must choose one of the Focus Areas: Environment, Disaster Alerts, Education, or Healthcare.

Round 2: Grand Finale

Only the Top 10 Teams from Round 1 will proceed to this stage.

  • Selected teams will receive an email invitation.
  • Re-registration Fee: 100 BDT (Required for finalists).
  • Format: Live Online Defense (Zoom/Google Meet).
  • Teams must answer questions from the judges regarding feasibility and impact.

Submission Guide

  1. Prepare your idea file (PDF/PPTX).
  2. Upload the file to your personal Google Drive.
  3. CRITICAL: Change the share setting to "Anyone with the link can view".
  4. Copy the link.
  5. Go to the website Submission section or use the Google Form provided.
  6. Paste your Drive link in the designated field.
