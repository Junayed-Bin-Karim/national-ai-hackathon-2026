The Problem

Traffic Congestion in Dhaka City

Dhaka loses 3.2 million working hours daily due to traffic jams. Traditional timer-based traffic lights fail to adapt to real-time vehicle density, causing unnecessary delays and increased CO2 emissions.

The AI Solution

Adaptive AI Traffic Control System

We propose a Computer Vision-based system that uses existing CCTV cameras to count vehicles in real-time. An AI model (YOLOv8) analyzes density and automatically adjusts traffic light duration.
